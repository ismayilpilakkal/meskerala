ABOUT MES

About us

The last five decades have been witnessing a remarkable and glorious transformation in the educational upbringing of the Muslims in Kerala, traditionally the most backward community in education. The driving force behind this envious achievement has been the committed and tiring toil of THE MUSLIM EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY (MES).

The movement started under the great leadership of late Dr. P.K.Abdul Gafoor in 1964 at Calicut, wholeheartedly supported by a large number of professionals and businessmen.

OUR JOURNEY

50 Years of Impact

1964

The Foundation

  • Started at Calicut under the leadership of Late Dr. P.K. Abdul Gafoor.
  • Supported by professionals and businessmen to uplift the educationally backward.
  • Initiated a massive awareness campaign to bring modern education to the community.
1994

A Revolution in Professional Education

  • Established MES College of Engineering at Kuttippuram.
  • The very first self-financing engineering college in Kerala's private sector.
  • Kick-started a historic revolution in the state to promote education to the deprived classes.
2003

Flagship Medical College

  • Established the MES Academy of Medical Sciences in Malappuram.
  • Attached a 500-bed charity hospital providing free medical services, diagnostics, and surgeries to the poor.
  • Currently benefits over 1 Lakh people per year through charity medical schemes.
2011

Post-Graduate Medical Institute

  • Elevated the Medical College to offer extensive PG degrees and diplomas across 15+ specialties.
  • MES Medicos consistently secure top university ranks, surpassing established government institutions.
2014

Golden Jubilee

  • Celebrated 50 years with a network of 150+ institutions across all 14 districts and the Middle East.
  • Reached over 10 Lakh direct and indirect beneficiaries.
  • Contributed significantly to Kerala's cent percent literacy rate and high modernization indices.
Present Day

Social Transformation

  • Elevated Muslim women's participation in education from 1% at inception to an astounding 70%.
  • Grew to a massive strength of 20,000 active members and 85,000 students.
  • Extended massive charitable activities and national medical missions (e.g., assisting refugees in Assam & UP).
True, MES has made tremendous achievements during a short spell of its existence thanks to the sacrifices as well as the committed and devoted ‘sweat and toil’ of thousands of active grass root level members of this prestigious organization. But we still feel that ‘ we have miles to go before we sleep…’ We carry on the mantle of our mission to uplift the backward segments of the people of our nation educationally, economically and socially so that these segments can also stand on their foot and take control of their due and worthy place in the mainstream of our great nation’s march forward to achieve and fulfill the great dream – any higher aspiration –: a society that both looks forward and onwards as well as holds itself to corrective standards for the whole people of our nation.
— Under the inspiring leadership of Dr. P.A. Fazal Ghafoor, President, MES
