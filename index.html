Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, courses, and campus life.

What is the admission process?
Admissions are generally merit-based or through entrance examinations, depending on the course. You can apply online through our official portal.
Are scholarships available?
Yes, MES offers various merit-based and need-based scholarships. Additionally, government scholarships are available for eligible students.
What about placements?
We have a dedicated placement cell with a track record of 90%+ placements in top MNCs, healthcare institutions, and corporate firms.
Are hostel facilities provided?
Yes, separate secure hostels for boys and girls are available across all major campuses with modern amenities and dining.
