FOUNDER OF MES KERALA

Late Dr. P.K. Abdul Gafoor

Visionary • Physician • Education Reformer

Late Dr. P.K. Abdul Gafoor founded the Muslim Educational Society (MES) in 1964 with a vision to make quality education accessible to all. His leadership transformed a single vision into one of India's most respected educational movements.

Family

Born to Padiyath Manapatt Kochu Moideen Haji and Karukappadath Kunchachumma. He came from a family that valued education and social service.

Education

Graduated from Aligarh Muslim University, secured his MBBS from Kerala University, and completed his DTM&H from Edinburgh, along with MRCP and FRCP.

Career

Served as a Tutor in Neurology, Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, and a dedicated Medical Educator across prestigious institutions.

Legacy

His farsighted vision continues to inspire MES and generations of students, leaving behind an institutional framework that stands the test of time.

MES Kerala

Committed to excellence in education since 1964, transforming society through knowledge, values, and innovation.
