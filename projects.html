Frequently Asked Questions

How can external organizations collaborate on projects?

We actively seek partnerships through our central Directorate of Projects. Organizations can reach out via the contact form to discuss joint research, CSR initiatives, or infrastructure development.

Are project funding reports publicly available?

Yes, we maintain complete transparency. Annual financial summaries of major projects are published in the Downloads section of this portal every fiscal year.

How do students get involved in innovation labs?

Students can apply for incubator space and project grants during our bi-annual 'Innovation Pitch Days' held across all major technical and arts campuses.
